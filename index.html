Company Mission

  • Mykori Dessert Café is the 21st century version of the traditional Japanese Dessert House in Malaysia and immediately gained popularity since 2016 from our first outlet which is located at Bandar Puchong Jaya.
  • Now in 2021, it is the market leader in the premium shaved ice dessert sector in Malaysia, with the most number of outlets (105 outlets) and amazing customer response (just check out our Instagram!)
  • In 2021, the most anticipated Mykori Dessert Café brand has now expanded to over 105 outlets nationwide in Malaysia
